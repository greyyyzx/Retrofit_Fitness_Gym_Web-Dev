Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?
We're open Monday to Saturday from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays to give our members time to rest.
Where is Retrofit Fitness Gym located?
You can find us in Angono, Rizal, Philippines. For more details, please visit our Contact Us page.
Do I need to book before visiting the gym?
Walk-ins are welcome anytime during our operating hours. You're free to drop by and start your workout whenever it's convenient for you. If you'd like extra guidance, our coach is available to assist you.
Do you offer discounts?
Yes, students enjoy special discounted rates. We also provide flexible options so you can choose what works best for your schedule and goals.
Do you offer personal training?
Yes, personal training is available. Our certified coach works with you one-on-one to create a plan that matches your goals, while providing guidance and support to help you build strength and improve fitness.
Do you offer personal training?
Yes! We offer group workouts that bring people together in a supportive and energetic environment. These sessions include functional training, endurance exercises, and community fitness activities.
Do you have showers and storage areas?
Yes, we provide shower facilities and secure storage areas where you can keep your belongings while you work out.
